About

The Unfound Project

An independent research initiative examining the structural architecture of missing persons investigations.

Overview

What this project is

The Unfound Project studies how missing persons investigations diverge through institutional structure rather than narrative mystery alone.

The project focuses on classification, response speed, evidence preservation, investigative clarity, public amplification, and long-term investigative persistence.

Approach

A restrained analytical method

The Unfound Project avoids sensationalism and treats cases as analytical illustrations of recurring structural patterns. The goal is not entertainment, but understanding.
