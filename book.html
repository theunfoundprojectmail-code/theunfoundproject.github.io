The Book

Unfound America

The Architecture of Missing Persons Response

This book is a systems-based examination of how institutional design influences missing persons investigations. Rather than retelling disappearances as isolated mystery, it studies the architecture that shapes investigative trajectory: classification, speed, preservation, visibility, and persistence.

The project uses structural analysis chapters, policy discussion, investigative process explanation, and case micro-studies to show that outcomes are influenced by institutional variables, not randomness alone.
